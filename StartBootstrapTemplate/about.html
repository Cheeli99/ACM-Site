About ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is an international organization, and the worlds largest computing society. The organization excels at bringing people together, acting as a hub for all things technology.

ACM at Wayne State College is just that. Get together with your classmates, and do some learning outside of the classroom! Complete group projects, go on trips and tours, and discuss and work with various technologies.
